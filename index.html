For our topic, we have chosen the inventors of popular programming languages, deciding on Brendan Eich, Gudio Van Rossum, James Gosling and Dennis Richie. We picked this for our theme as programming languages have had and still do have a huge impact on the computing industry and, by extension, so have the people responsible for designing and developing these languages.

In the 21st century, almost every electronic device uses a programming language in some way, including mobile phones, computers, and cars.
